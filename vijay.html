vijay

"Vijay" often refers to Thalapathy Vijay (Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar), a superstar Tamil actor known for commercial blockbusters, who recently launched a political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), and is transitioning from acting to politics. Other notable figures with the name include actor Duniya Vijay, producer/actor Vijay Sethupathi, director A. L. Vijay, and Paytm founder Vijay Shekhar Sharma, but the actor Vijay is currently most prominent due to his political entry.
